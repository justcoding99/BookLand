About Us

BookLand is a online library for the intellectually curious. We help everyone discover, share, and connect valuable ideas. BookLand is a great resource for locating unique, trustworthy books that you often can’t find anywhere except in a library. And by connecting thousands of book collections in one place, BookLand makes it easy for you to browse the world’s libraries from one easy search box. Like a paper library, we provide free access to researchers, historians, scholars, people with print disabilities, and the general public. Our mission is to provide universal access to all knowledge.
